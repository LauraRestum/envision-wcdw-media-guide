Language that respects the community

Quick rephrases

A handful of swaps keep everything on-message. When in doubt: lead with independence, center the person.

“visually impaired,” “the blind,” “BVI”→people who are blind or have low vision
“Envision Dallas,” a foundation name→Envision (Envision is the organization; “Envision in North Texas” for context)
“suffers from vision loss”→“is blind or has low vision” (capability, never pity)
“Join us at the Dallas Zoo!”→“Walk with us for independence.” (the Zoo is the setting, never the pitch)
“3rd Annual White Cane Day Walk”→White Cane Day Walk (no annual numbering)
“helped 1,200 people last year”→“serves thousands of North Texans locally each year” (the only approved stat)
“jobs, education and research”→employment, outreach, rehabilitation, education and research (always these five, in this order)
