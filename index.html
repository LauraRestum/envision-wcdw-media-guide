Language that respects the community

Quick rephrases

A handful of swaps keep everything on-message. When in doubt: lead with independence, center the person.

“visually impaired,” “the blind,” “BVI”→people who are blind or have low vision
“Envision Dallas”→Envision (“Envision in North Texas” for context)
“suffers from vision loss”→“is blind or has low vision” (agency, not pity)
“Join us at the Dallas Zoo!”→“Walk with us for independence.” (the Zoo is the venue, not the story)
“the Envision Dallas Foundation”→Envision's Dallas Foundation (with the apostrophe)
